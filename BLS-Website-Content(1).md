## 🚀 Products / Portfolio Section

**Section Label:** What We've Built
**Headline:** Our Products

---

### SaccoOS
Multi-tenant SACCO management platform for savings and credit cooperatives across Uganda.
**Stack:** Next.js · Supabase · PostgreSQL
**Features:** Member management, loan processing, repayment tracking, multi-tenant architecture

---

### DukaPOS
Point-of-sale system built for Ugandan shops — works offline, prints thermal receipts, and tracks inventory in real time.
**Stack:** Next.js · Drizzle ORM · PWA
**Features:** Offline-first, thermal receipt printing, inventory management, EgoSMS integration

---

### Skolia
School management system for Ugandan schools with QR attendance, WhatsApp report cards, and MoMo fee payments.
**Stack:** Next.js · Supabase · Green API
**Features:** QR-based attendance, dynamically generated report cards, WhatsApp PDF delivery, MTN MoMo payments

---

### VerifyLink
SMS OTP authentication SaaS for developers and businesses operating in Uganda and East Africa.
**Stack:** FastAPI · Next.js · EgoSMS · Supabase
**Features:** SMS OTP, developer SDK, real-time logs, usage dashboard

---

### OpenDeliver
Last-mile delivery orchestration platform designed for the East African logistics market.
**Stack:** Next.js · Capacitor.js · Supabase · OSRM
**Features:** Multi-tenant, route optimization, mobile money payouts, real-time driver tracking
